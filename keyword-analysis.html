Phase 2: Understanding Key Metrics
  • Cerebro IQ Score: A proprietary metric indicating a keyword's overall attractiveness and effectiveness, where higher scores suggest greater potential value.
  • Search Volume: Represents the estimated average monthly number of searches for a specific keyword. Higher volume indicates greater potential visibility and demand.
  • Search Volume Trend: Illustrates the recent change in a keyword's search volume. A positive trend indicates growing interest, while a negative trend suggests decreasing interest.
  • Competing Products: The total number of products currently indexed for a given keyword. Lower numbers typically suggest less direct competition.
  • Title Density: The number of competing products that explicitly include the exact keyword phrase in their product title. A lower density indicates less direct competition for title optimization, potentially offering easier ranking opportunities.
